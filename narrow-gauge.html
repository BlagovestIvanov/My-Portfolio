The Rhodope Narrow Gauge

This is one of my favorite photos. The last narrow-gauge in Bulgaria. I took the picture on the outskirts of Dobrinishte. The train route passes through the picturesque gorge of Chepinska River. The starting point of the line is the town of Septemvri and the final is Dobrinishte, where I took the picture. Both cities are very nice and beautiful places. I highly recommend it to any nature lover.
