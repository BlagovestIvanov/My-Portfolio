View From The Rocks Over Lakatnik

On this photo you can see one of the most beautiful places in Bulgaria - the view from the rocks over Lakatnik station. The place is not difficult to access and reveals an incredible view. From here you can see the whole village, the railway station and the Iskar River, which is responsible for the beauty of these rocks. I recommend everyone at least once to visit this place and enjoy it.
